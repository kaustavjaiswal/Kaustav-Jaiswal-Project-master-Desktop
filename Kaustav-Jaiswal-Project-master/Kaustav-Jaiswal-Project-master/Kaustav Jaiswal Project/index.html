SRM University is one of the top ranking universities in India with over 38,000 students and more than 2600 faculty across all the campus, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health sciences, and Science and Humanities.

Vision To emerge as a World - Class University in creating and disseminating knowledge, and providing students a unique learning experience in Science, Technology, Medicine, Management and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the world and betterment of mankind. Mission MOVE UP through international alliances and collaborative initiatives to achieve global excellence. ACCOMPLISH A PROCESS to advance knowledge in a rigorous academic and research environment. ATTRACT AND BUILD PEOPLE in a rewarding and inspiring environment by fostering freedom, empowerment, creativity and innovation.

SRM University is headed by the Chancellor, supported by the Chairman Mr. Ravi Pachamoothoo, the President Dr. P. Sathyanarayanan, the Vice - President Dr. R. Shivakumar and the Vice - Chancellor(In-Charge) Dr. T. P. Ganesan. The Directors of Faculties, Admission, Corporate Affairs and Student Mentoring, report directly to the Vice - Chancellor. Divisions like planning, finance and legal, work closely with Vice Chancellor reporting to the Board of Management. The three faculties - Engineering & Technology, Medical & Health Sciences, and Science & Humanities - are headed by Directors. The key functional areas including Research, Admission, Corporate Affairs and Student Mentoring are headed by directors. The various Schools are headed by Deans and the Departments by HODs.

Ministry of HRD places SRM University in Category A The Ministry of Human Resource Development(MHRD), Department of Higher Education, Government of India, has placed SRM University in highest Category 'A'. This is indeed a proud moment for SRM University.

SRM is committed to creation of knowledge through research across all academic disciplines. Focus areas include Nanotechnology, Bio engineering, Info, Energy, Environment, Materials and Embedded systems. Joint research project on rural healthcare, in collaboration with Queen's University, Canada.
